1 Overview

Welcome to Football Bros ("we," "us," or "our"), accessible at https://footballbros-io.github.io/. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website and play Football Bros online.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Football Bros is a free browser-based gaming website. We do not sell your personal data, run advertising networks, or require account creation to use our service.

🏈 Short version: We collect minimal data, we don't sell it, and you can play Football Bros without creating an account or providing any personal information.

By accessing or using Football Bros, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you disagree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of the site.

2 Data We Collect

Information You Provide

Football Bros does not require you to create an account or submit any personal information to play the game. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily contact us.

Information Collected Automatically

Like most websites, our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) may automatically collect certain technical information when you visit the site, including:

  • Your IP address (anonymised by GitHub's infrastructure)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring URL (the page that linked you here)
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Date and time of your visit

This data is collected automatically by the GitHub Pages infrastructure and is used solely for the operation and security of the hosting service. We do not have direct access to raw server logs at the individual-user level.

Star Ratings

The star rating widget on our homepage is processed entirely within your browser session. Rating data is not transmitted to any server and is not stored beyond your current visit.

3 Cookies & Local Storage

Football Bros does not use its own tracking cookies or advertising cookies. Our website itself does not set any first-party cookies on your device.

However, the following may result in cookies or storage being set:

  • Google Fonts: We load web fonts from Google Fonts. Google may set a short-lived cookie to optimise font delivery. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Embedded game (iframe): The Football Bros game is served from a third-party domain. That provider may use cookies or local storage for game state and session management. Please refer to Section 5 for more details.
  • GitHub Pages: GitHub, our hosting provider, may use cookies for security and infrastructure purposes.

You can control cookie behaviour through your browser settings. Most modern browsers allow you to block, delete, or get notifications about cookies. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the embedded game.

4 Third-Party Services

Football Bros relies on a small number of third-party services to deliver the website. Each third party operates under its own privacy policy, which we encourage you to review:

  • GitHub Pages – Website hosting. GitHub Privacy Statement
  • Google Fonts – Web font delivery. Google Privacy Policy
  • UnblockedRush.com – Game hosting provider (iframe source). Please see their privacy policy directly on their website.

We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any other advertising or behavioural tracking technologies on this site.

5 Embedded Game (iframe)

The Football Bros game is embedded on this page via an <iframe> that loads content from unblockedrush.com. When you interact with the game, your browser communicates directly with their servers.

We do not control the data practices of UnblockedRush.com. Their platform may:

  • Collect your IP address and browser information for game delivery
  • Store game progress or settings in your browser's local storage or cookies
  • Display advertisements within the game environment
⚠️ Note: If you prefer not to have data collected by the game provider, you may choose not to interact with the embedded game iframe.

We recommend reviewing UnblockedRush.com's privacy policy for complete details about their data practices.

6 Children's Privacy

Football Bros is an all-ages game, and our website is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction).

Because we do not collect personal information through our own site, we believe our service is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us at the details below and we will promptly address it.

Parents should be aware that the embedded game from UnblockedRush.com may have separate data practices. We encourage parents to review their policy for any child-specific concerns.

7 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding any personal data that may be associated with your use of this site:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Object: Object to certain processing of your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.
