How to Play Football Bros

The objective in Football Bros is straightforward: carry the football into your opponent's end zone to score a touchdown while preventing them from doing the same to you. The player controlling the football must sprint, dodge, and juke to avoid tackles. The defending player must time their moves to intercept and stop the ball carrier.

  • Player 1 controls: W (up), A (left), S (down), D (right)
  • Player 2 controls: Arrow Up, Arrow Left, Arrow Down, Arrow Right
  • First player to reach the target score wins the match
  • Collect power-ups on the field for temporary boosts and advantages

Football Bros Tips & Strategy

Mastering Football Bros goes beyond just running in a straight line. Here are some key strategies:

  • Cut back: Fake one direction then dash the opposite way to leave defenders behind.
  • Power-up priority: Some power-ups provide massive speed boosts – prioritise them when you're on defense and running down the ball carrier.
  • Corner pressure: Back your opponent into a corner of the field where their movement options are limited.
  • Patience: Don't always sprint – sometimes slowing down to bait a tackle attempt creates the opening you need.
